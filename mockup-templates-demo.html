What are Sale Templates?

Sale Templates let you save and reuse common product combinations that you frequently sell together. Instead of selecting products one by one, you can use a template to add multiple products to a sale instantly!

  • Faster Checkout: Sell bundles with one click
  • Reduce Errors: Pre-configured quantities and prices
  • Track Bundles: Know which product combinations are popular
  • Better Customer Service: Quick service for repeat customers
